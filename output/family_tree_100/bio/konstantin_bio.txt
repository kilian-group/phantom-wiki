Konstantin Stone is a Sound Technician specializing in broadcasting, film, and
video. With over a decade of experience,  Konstantin has contributed to projects
at Echo Studios and Sonic Wave Productions,  bringing a deep understanding of
sound mixing and editing.  His expertise with industry-standard tools like
AcouSound Pro and AudioMix Studio is acclaimed. In his free time, Konstantin
enjoys composing ambient music and experimenting with soundscapes.  He aims to
push the boundaries of audio production in multimedia projects.

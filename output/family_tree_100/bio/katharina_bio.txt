Katharina Evans is a renowned Child Psychotherapist known for her compassionate
approach and innovative techniques in therapy.  She completed her Master's in
Child Psychology from Eastwood University followed by specialized training in
psychotherapy. Over the past 25 years, Katharina has worked with children facing
various emotional and psychological challenges.  Her techniques are especially
impactful in helping children cope with trauma, cognitive behavioral therapy
being her area of expertise. Katharina resides in Lakewood where she runs a
successful private practice.  She lives with her partner, Casey and their two
imaginative children.  In her free time, Katharina loves to paint murals and is
an avid music enthusiast.

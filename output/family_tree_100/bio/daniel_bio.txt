Daniel is a dedicated speech and language therapist based in Treeville. He
earned his Bachelor of Science in Communication Disorders , holding a degree
from Whispering Pines University . He is passionate about working at Voiceland
Therapy Center , where he helps individuals overcome communication challenges.
With over ten  years of experience, Daniel specializes in pediatric speech
therapy and adult neurological speech disorders . In his free time, he enjoys
reading historical novels and hiking the trails of Emerald Forest .

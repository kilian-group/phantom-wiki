Luisa Greenwood is a seasoned Tree Surgeon who specializes in unique
arboricultural practices.  She owns and operates the renowned firm, Verdant
Canopy Solutions , which is celebrated for pioneering eco-friendly tree
management strategies.  With a deep-rooted passion for nature, she pursued a
degree in Environmental Horticulture from Elmsworth University and has been
nurturing trees ever since.   In her personal life, Luisa enjoys birdwatching
and painting landscape art and lives in Willowbrook Heights with her beloved
rescue dog, Darby.

Meet Sebastian Silver, a dedicated Conservator, furniture from Willow Creek ,
with over 15 years of experience in preserving the craft. He has worked on
various high-profile restoration projects, including collaborations with the
renowned Velarian Museum and the illustrious Lancelot Galleries. Sebastian is
also an avid traveler and an enthusiastic collector of rare antiques. His
passion for conservation, combined with his eye for detail, makes him a valuable
asset in the world of art preservation.

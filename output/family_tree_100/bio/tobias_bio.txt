Tobias is a talented Sound Technician specializing in broadcasting, film, and
video. He currently works for SonicWave Productions, where he has honed his
skills over the past 5 years. With an exceptional ear for detail, Tobias has
worked on notable projects such as the hit films 'Echoes of Silence' and
'Rhythms of the Night.' Outside of work, he is passionate about exploring new
audio technologies and experimenting with soundscapes. Tobias continues to push
the boundaries of sound in every project he undertakes.

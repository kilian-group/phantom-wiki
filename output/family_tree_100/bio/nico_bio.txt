Nico  hails from the vibrant town of  Greenwood  and has always been passionate
about helping people and plants connect.  A seasoned Horticultural therapist,
Nico has spent over  five  years working at  Greenpath Rehabilitation Center
where they specialize in therapeutic gardening.  In their free time, Nico enjoys
writing nature poetry  and exploring local botanical gardens.  Believing in the
healing power of nature, Nico is committed to fostering resilience and joy
through horticulture.

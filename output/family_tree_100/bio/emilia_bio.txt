Emilia Stone  is an Engineering Geologist at EarthQuest Solutions.  She
graduated with a degree in Geological Engineering from Stellar University, where
she developed a strong interest in tectonic activities and rock mechanics.  Over
her career, Emilia has worked on various high-profile projects, including the
assessment of landslide risks for the Lunar Nova infrastructure project and the
stability analysis of the Titan Tunnel.  In her leisure time, Emilia enjoys
hiking, often exploring the ancient trails of the Terra Shade Mountains. She is
also an avid painter, merging her passion for geology and art through vibrant
landscape paintings.

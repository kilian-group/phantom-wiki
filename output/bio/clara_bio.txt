Meet Clara, a renowned futures trader with a knack for navigating the
complexities of financial markets. Clara graduated with a degree in Economics
from Flotwin University, where she honed her analytical skills.  Originally from
the bustling city of New Aradia, she learned the importance of adaptability and
foresight early on. Starting her career at the prestigious brokerage firm,
Sterling & Blythe,  Clara quickly gained recognition for her innovative trading
strategies and her ability to predict market trends with remarkable accuracy.
When she's not engrossed in the world of futures, Clara enjoys hiking in the
serene mountains of Verduin and exploring contemporary art galleries.

Michael is a skilled and dedicated Town Planner from Palmdale.  He earned his
undergraduate degree in Urban Development from Greenfield University.  Michael
furthered his studies with a Master's in Spatial Planning at Elmbridge
Institute.  He has worked for over a decade at Riverstone Planning Agency where
he has been an integral part of the city planning team.  His notable achievement
includes the successful redesign of the Oakridge Commercial District, which won
the prestigious Horizon Award.  Outside of work, Michael is a devoted family
man, residing in Palmdale with his spouse, Alex, and their two children.  In his
free time, Michael enjoys exploring hiking trails, playing chess at the local
club, and volunteering for community clean-up events.
